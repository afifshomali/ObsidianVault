## Case $(I)$, $x \leq 0$
---
$$f(x) = (x-a)^2 - \eta x $$
$$f'(x) = 2(x -a) - \eta = 2 [x - (a + \dfrac{\eta}{2})] $$
## Case $(II)$, $x > 0$
---
$$ f'(x) = 2 [x - (a - \dfrac{\eta}{2})]$$
